Covid-19

In this project, we take a dive into the information provided by Our World In Data that contains recorded cases, tests, vaccinations, etc... by date and country. The project consists of plots that illustrates the trend in these variables over the course of 3 years with a forecast predicting the likelihood of the future trend into 2.5 years and statistics geographically portraying information such as death rates and positivity rates.
